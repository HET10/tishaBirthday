Poems for You

A little snippet of how I feel about you.

I. Prismed Void

The firmament fell heavy, void of sound,
As ancient sorrows tethered me to ground.
I drifted through the ruin, vast and stark,
A fractured vessel drowning in the dark.

Then broke the dawn through fissures in the grey,
You turned the obsidian night to radiant day.
With artisan hands, you mended every tear,
And banished all the specters of my fear.

Now in your luminous orbit I reside,
With nowhere left for shadows to abide.
My iris, bearing colors distinct and true,
I find my infinite worth reflected in you.

II. Gilded Echo

Existence was a cadence, dull and lead,
A library of heavy words unsaid.
But you arrived with laughter, bright and fierce,
The armor of my cynicism to pierce.

You taught the stone to dance, the mute to sing,
Unveiling joy that only you could bring.
A spirit woven from the silk of light,
You forced the grim despondency to flight.

Beneath your gaze, the dissonance shall cease,
Replaced by rhythms of a perfect peace.
I stand worthier now, within your scope,
A man baptized in happiness and hope.

III. Blooming Ash

I wandered through a maze of entropy,
Where chaos was the only thing to see.
The pillars of my life had turned to dust,
Corroded by the failure and the rust.

But then you flowered, vibrant and sublime,
Defying all the cruelties of time.
My iris, arching distinct amidst the rain,
You washed away the sediment of pain.

Your presence is a fortress, strong and deep,
Where I am safe before I fall asleep.
You are the reason that my soul creates,
The love that opens up the golden gates.
